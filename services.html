RESPITE CARE
Caring dor someone can be very rewarding and it can aslo be demanding and tiring work - both physically and emotionally.
Order

It is important thar family carers take time off to have a break and to look after their own health and wellbeing. This can happen through respite care. Our Respite care is a form of support for carers. Our Respite Care may be for a few hours a day as it provides the opportunity to attend to everyday activities and have a break from the caring role. We can also take the person that is being cared on an outing for a few hours whilst being able to tend to things around the home. Our Respite Care can be provided:

  • At any time during the day or evening
  • Overnight
  • Weekdays
  • Weekends

It can also be provided for longer periods such as when family members need to take a well-earned break or go on holidays.

Respite care can also be a positive experience for the person receiving care it also provides assurance that they are being professionally cared for and in safe hands.
